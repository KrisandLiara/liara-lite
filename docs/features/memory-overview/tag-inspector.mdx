# Tag Inspector

The Tag Inspector opens as a centered glassy panel within the content area. It closes when clicking outside or pressing Escape.

## Contents

- Summary: total matches, first/last seen
- Co‑facets: top 5 per bucket (people, orgs, products, locations, keywords)
- Timeline: monthly counts (YYYY‑MM)
- Sources: chat vs other
- Examples: newest five with title/snippet

## Tips

- Click co‑facet chips to AND them to your selection
- Shift+Click to exclude a co‑facet
- Use the search bar to intersect keyword filtering with your selection
